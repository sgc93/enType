enType

enType is an ease to use and customizable typing test with different interesting features. it features modes - test with English and Programming Language keywords, about 4 different themes and sample shortcuts to restart game, toggle between themes, ... It enables you to test you typing skills under different modes, and If you are a programmer, lucky for you, there are around 6 modes for 6 programming languages to type on and be familiar and explore their keywords.

Eas To Use

Different Levels

Calculation

In simple terms, enType measures your typing speed by filtering and counting number of correct words typed and divide by the chosen test duration

Shortcuts

enType includes about 4 shortcuts: to restart game - Enter Key, to toggle between themes - ArrowUp key, to open and close about modal window - Esc Key and to show/close contact modal window - Ctrl Key
